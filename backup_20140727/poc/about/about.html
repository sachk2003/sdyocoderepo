About Us

SuperDealyo was founded in 2014 to bring location aware offerings of products and services to your fingertips. The menu allows users to create shopping lists that mimics human shopping behavior and the results are given in a format that allow easy comparision of deals. One can also search by a specific vendor in a given location. Users can access the system by web browsers or apps using variety of devices including laptops, tablets and smart phones. The location can be manually entered or can be auto-detected. The offerings cover perishable and non-perishable products as well as multitude of services offered by vendors. Users can also create profiles to receive alerts. These game changing features differentiate SuperDealyo system from store websites, auction websites, online store websites, other apps, group coupon websites, TV, radio, yellow pages and print media circulars. SuperDealyo system handles plurality of vendors, users and offerings by using a dynamic and flexible approach to data in the database system and data accuracy is ensured by harmonizing data with industry standards.

The company invests a significant amount of its resources in building this uniqueplatform that brings consumers, vendors and product manufacturers together. This improves shopping experience for all.

The company operates globally, headquartered in Princeton Juntion, NJ, USA.
