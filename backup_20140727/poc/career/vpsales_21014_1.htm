TITLE: Vice President Sales & Marketing

REPORTING RELATIONSHIP: The Vice President of Sales & Marketing will report to the CEO.

DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:

S/he will be responsible for the following areas:

  • Take ownership of, and direct the Sales & Marketing strategy.
  • Executing on the sales strategy would include the following tasks: Drive revenue generating sales opportunities with customers, develop and implement a plan to sustain and increase revenue and market share to the company, drive key lead generation programs, close sales with customers, manage inside sales, interact with systems engineering.
  • Enhance and grow the sales organization through management and leadership.
  • Conduct high-level customer presentations and relationship-building with key customers.
  • Recruit and develop the sales organization.

THE CANDIDATE

BACKGROUND AND EXPERIENCE:

  • A solid track record in successfully driving sales opportunities to closure with software products. Minimum of 10-12 years experience in software sales. Experience in developing and executing maketing strategies using social media and other types of media is required. Strong sales and marketing background in It systems related to consumer products is highly desirable. Strong analytical and business deal making skills, able to generate opportunities, create positive relationships, find the hidden issues during due diligence and bring the transition to closure successfully.
  • Proven ability to plan and execute: an ability to develop a coherent, forward-looking sales strategy, to create business opportunities and close deals.
  • Ability to effectively sell company vision and corporate direction, coupled with the ability to create interest and excitement around a value proposition and product vision.
  • Strong skills in relationship development. Ability to gain entry to and recognition by the appropriate levels of management. Strong network of personal contacts in the industry.
  • Organizational leadership ability; able to hire strong people, manage them to high levels of performance, and to establish effective and efficient processes.
  • Experience in driving sales from the ground up would be a plus.

EDUCATION:

The candidate should have a business or technical degree. An advanced degree would be a plus, but experience and accomplishments are much more important.

PERSONAL CHARACTERSTICS:

Coupled with one's experience are the personal qualities which determine an individual's ability to make an impact and be credible with internal and external audiences.

To fit, the candidate must be a proven sales executive. S/he must be a skilled communicator, both orally and in writing, and display self-confidence and high energy. The successful candidate will be resourceful, self-motivated and a highly responsive individual. The candidate will have proven skills managing people, be hands-on, and feel comfortable in a hard charging, ever changing entrepreneurial environment.
